Fast Folders Privacy Policy

Updated: 04/03/26

Your privacy is important to me! At Fast Folders, your privacy is a top priority. This extension is developed and maintained by an independent developer. I am committed to protecting your data and complying with applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR.

Data Controller


Fast Folders is the data controller. Contact: fastfolders@protonmail.com

What Fast Folders Stores (And Where)


Local data (always): When you use Fast Folders without cloud sync, all data stays on your device using the browser's local storage:

  • Folder names, folder colours, and structures
  • Prompts, tags, and Fast Folder settings
  • Saved chat names and associated URLs

Cloud sync data (optional): If you enable Fast Folders Cloud, the following is stored on our servers to sync across your devices:

  • Your email address (for authentication via magic link)
  • Folder names, structures, and order
  • Saved prompts and prompt tags
  • Settings (e.g. dark mode, sidebar position)

Purpose and Lawful Basis


We process your data to provide the extension and, when you opt in, cloud sync. The lawful basis is performance of our contract with you and, where applicable, your consent.

What Fast Folders Does Not Do


Fast Folders does not use analytics or cookies. When using local storage only, no data is transmitted to external servers. Fast Folders does not access your browser history or any unrelated browser activity.

Data Processors


Cloud sync data is processed by Supabase. Supabase acts as our data processor. Their privacy policy and sub-processors apply: supabase.com/privacy

Retention


Cloud sync data is retained while your account is active. When you sign out and delete your account or request deletion, we will delete your data within a reasonable period.

Your Rights (GDPR)


If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of your data
  • Rectification – correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure – request deletion of your data
  • Portability – receive your data in a portable format
  • Object – object to processing
  • Withdraw consent – where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact fastfolders@protonmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

Unlimited Storage Permission


Fast Folders requests the "unlimitedStorage" permission in order to:

  • Allow you to create and store a large number of folders and chat entries
  • Prevent storage-related issues or data loss

This permission only increases storage capacity; it does not allow access to other browser data. Your data stays on your device (or in your cloud account if you use sync); I do not access it.

Automatic Removal


When you uninstall Fast Folders:

  • All local data is automatically removed from your computer
  • No local data remains on your system
  • Cloud data remains until you request deletion
  • You may be redirected to an uninstall feedback form

Updates


This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted here and, where appropriate, communicated to you. Updates will also be reflected on the official extension listing or documentation page.

Contact


If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, feel free to reach out: fastfolders@protonmail.com
